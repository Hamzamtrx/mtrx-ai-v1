## COPY SKILL â€” TYPE 4

### Headline Formula
**Structure:** [PRODUCT]. [COMPARISON]. â€” Direct, factual, bold

**Comparison Types:**

1. **Greater Than:** "[YOUR MATERIAL] > [COMPETITOR MATERIAL]"
   - "HEMP > POLYESTER"
   - "ORGANIC > SYNTHETIC"
   - "REAL > FAKE"

2. **Simple Statement:** "[PRODUCT TYPE]. [ONE WORD BENEFIT]."
   - "HEMP T-SHIRT. INDESTRUCTIBLE."
   - "THE HOODIE. UPGRADED."

3. **Question Challenge:** "WHY ARE YOU STILL WEARING [OLD THING]?"
   - "WHY ARE YOU STILL WEARING PLASTIC?"
   - "STILL BUYING FAST FASHION?"

**Rules:**
- 2-5 words per line
- ALL CAPS
- Period after each statement (authority)
- Creates clear contrast or challenge

### Benefit Badges
**Structure:** [SHORT BENEFIT] â€” 2-3 words max, pill/badge shape

**Categories of Benefits:**

**Material:**
- "ALL NATURAL"
- "100% HEMP"
- "ORGANIC COTTON"

**Environmental:**
- "ZERO MICROPLASTICS"
- "BIODEGRADABLE"
- "PLANET FRIENDLY"

**Durability:**
- "LIFETIME GUARANTEE"
- "10X STRONGER"
- "WON'T SHRINK"

**Comfort:**
- "ODOR RESISTANT"
- "BREATHABLE"
- "TEMPERATURE REGULATING"

**Rules:**
- 3 badges maximum (information overload otherwise)
- ALL CAPS
- Warm colors (cream, tan, gold) on illustrated backgrounds
- Stack vertically or horizontal row

### CTA Line
**Structure:** [ACTION] + [BRAND IDENTITY]

**Examples:**
- "BECOME AN UNDRDOG TODAY"
- "JOIN THE HEMP REVOLUTION"
- "UPGRADE YOUR WARDROBE"
- "SHOP THE DROP"
- "DITCH THE PLASTIC"

**Rules:**
- Creates identity/tribe feeling
- ALL CAPS
- Bold banner or button style

## ILLUSTRATION STYLES

### Style A: Backyard/Outdoor Scene
Fence, grass, sky with sun rays.
Characters standing in yard.
Casual, relatable, "everyday" feel.

### Style B: Abstract/Gradient
Colorful gradient background.
Product floating in space.
Modern, clean, less narrative.

### Style C: Split Scene
Left side: gray/dull (before/competitor)
Right side: bright/vibrant (after/product)
Clear transformation visual.

### Style D: Infographic Comic
White background, comic panel style.
Characters explaining benefits.
Educational but fun.
