## COPY SKILL — TYPE 3

### Product Code/Name Line
**Structure:** [CODE]: [NAME] — creates collectible/exclusive feel

**Examples:**
- "007: AGENT GOLD"
- "001: MIDNIGHT BLACK"
- "CORE COLLECTION: SLATE"
- "LIMITED: VINTAGE WASH"
- "NEW DROP: FOREST GREEN"

**Rules:**
- Short, punchy
- Creates product identity
- Accent color (orange, gold, red)
- ALL CAPS

### Announcement Headline
**Structure:** [STATUS/ACTION] — maximum 3 words

**Examples:**
- "WE ARE LIVE"
- "NOW AVAILABLE"
- "SOLD OUT" (with waitlist CTA)
- "BACK IN STOCK"
- "DROPPING FRIDAY"
- "FINAL RESTOCK"

**Rules:**
- 2-3 words maximum
- ALL CAPS
- Bold, dominant
- Creates urgency/excitement

### Badge/Label
**Structure:** [URGENCY/SCARCITY INDICATOR]

**Examples:**
- "LIMITED EDITION"
- "ONLY 500 MADE"
- "FIRST DROP"
- "MEMBER EXCLUSIVE"
- "24 HOURS ONLY"
- "WAITLIST OPEN"

**Rules:**
- Rectangular badge shape
- Accent color background
- Creates FOMO
- ALL CAPS

## PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY STYLES

### Style A: Folded Stack
Multiple units folded and stacked, showing depth/quantity.
Top item shows tag. Suggests "fresh from production."

### Style B: Flat Lay Spread
Product laid flat on textured surface.
Details visible — seams, labels, fabric texture.

### Style C: Hanging Hero
Single product on hanger against textured backdrop.
Full silhouette visible.

### Style D: Rolled/Bundled
Products rolled or bundled together.
Gift-like presentation.
