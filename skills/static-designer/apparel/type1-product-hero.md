## COPY SKILL — TYPE 1

### Headline Formula
**Structure:** [SUPERLATIVE] + [PRODUCT] + [YOU'LL EVER] + [VERB]

**Examples:**
- "THE STRONGEST SHIRT YOU'LL EVER OWN."
- "THE LAST T-SHIRT YOU'LL EVER BUY."
- "THE ONLY HOODIE YOU'LL EVER NEED."
- "THE TOUGHEST TEE ON THE PLANET."

**Rules:**
- 3-7 words max
- ALL CAPS
- End with period for authority
- Focus on ONE dominant claim (strongest, last, only, toughest)

### Offer Line Formula
**Structure:** ✓ + [Benefit/Guarantee]

**Examples:**
- "✓ Lifetime Guarantee"
- "✓ Free Shipping Over $50"
- "✓ 100-Day Risk-Free Trial"
- "✓ Made in USA"

**Rules:**
- Single line
- Checkmark prefix
- Accent color (brand secondary — often orange, gold, or green)

### CTA Options
- SHOP NOW
- GET YOURS
- BUY NOW
- CLAIM YOURS
