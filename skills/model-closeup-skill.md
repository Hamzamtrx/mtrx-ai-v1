# MODEL CLOSE-UP SKILL

**Status:** VALIDATED ✅  
**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026

Generate professional close-up beauty/product shots — face + product, tight framing.

---

## WHEN TO USE

- Beauty/skincare product ads
- Premium supplement marketing
- "This is my secret" intimate feel
- Face-focused product presentation

---

## BRAND-MATCHED EXPRESSION

```
NURTURING / MOM / WELLNESS BRANDS:
→ Warm smile showing teeth
→ Approachable, friendly, trustworthy
→ Eye crinkles (genuine smile)
→ "I want to share this with you" energy

LUXURY / PREMIUM BRANDS:
→ Confident soft expression, minimal smile
→ Quiet confidence, assured
→ Subtle closed-mouth smile
→ "I know my worth" energy

CLINICAL / PROFESSIONAL BRANDS:
→ Professional calm smile
→ Reassuring, credible
→ Soft closed-mouth smile
```

---

## TEMPLATE: CLOSE-UP (WARM — for nurturing brands)

```
Commercial beauty product photography for wellness supplement brand.

REFERENCE IMAGE:
Use the 3x3 product grid. SELECT THE FRONT HERO ANGLE (top-left).
CLONE the [PRODUCT] design EXACTLY.

ADVERTISEMENT TYPE: Clean beauty/wellness brand campaign. Professional, warm, approachable.

THIS IS A COMMERCIAL WELLNESS AD — PROFESSIONAL, BRAND-SAFE.

MODEL:
Woman, early 30s, healthy radiant complexion, warm [skin tone].
Dark hair neatly styled behind ears — face clearly visible.
Natural makeup — groomed brows, natural lip color, healthy glow.
OUTFIT: Cream knit sweater visible at neckline. Modest, professional.

EXPRESSION — WARM AND APPROACHABLE:
Looking at camera with warm confidence.
SOFT SMILE SHOWING TEETH — natural, relaxed, genuine.
Not a big laugh, not a grin — a warm "I'm happy to share this with you" smile.
Eyes engaged and friendly — slight crinkle at corners.
Approachable, trustworthy energy.
Like she's about to recommend this to a friend.

FRAMING:
Head and shoulders portrait — tight but professional.
Face takes 50-60% of frame.
Product held at cheek/face height with one hand.
Professional beauty shot framing.

PRODUCT PLACEMENT:
Product held near face/cheek with one hand.
Product angled so design faces camera.
Product clearly visible next to face.

HANDS — EXACTLY 1 HAND HOLDING PRODUCT:
Only one hand visible in frame — holding product.
Other arm out of frame.
Hand: natural feminine hand, neat natural nails.
Relaxed elegant grip.
EXACTLY 5 fingers.

LIGHTING — PROFESSIONAL BEAUTY:
Soft studio lighting from front-left.
Clean, bright, flattering.
Warm tone — inviting, not clinical.
Slight shadow on right side of face for dimension.

SKIN REALISM — CRITICAL:
Real skin texture — visible pores on nose, cheeks, forehead.
Natural skin variation — NOT uniform smooth.
Smile lines visible when smiling.
Subtle under-eye tone (not dark circles, just natural).
Healthy glow, not matte flat, not airbrushed.
Like unedited iPhone photo of beautiful person.

HAIR REALISM:
Baby hairs at hairline.
Flyaways catching light.
Natural movement, not perfectly styled.
NOT helmet hair.

BACKGROUND:
Warm beige seamless studio backdrop.
Clean, professional.

PRODUCT:
[PRODUCT DESCRIPTION — be specific about colors, text, design elements]

CAMERA:
85mm. f/2.8. Professional portrait.

OVERALL FEEL:
Warm, approachable, confident.
"I found something great and want to share it" energy.
Premium wellness, friendly not cold.

NOT: Stern expression, closed mouth, cold/distant, neutral flat expression, overly serious, unfriendly, stock photo stiff, flat lighting, airbrushed skin, helmet hair, extra hands.
```

## PRODUCT LOCK (if fidelity issues)

If product keeps simplifying, add this at TOP of prompt:

```
PRODUCT DESIGN — READ THIS 3 TIMES BEFORE GENERATING:

The product has THIS EXACT DESIGN:
- Base color: [COLOR]
- TOP SECTION: [describe]
- MIDDLE SECTION: [main text, logo]
- BOTTOM SECTION: [color, tagline]

THIS IS NOT A CLEAN MINIMAL DESIGN.
[Describe visual complexity]

COPY THE REFERENCE GRID IMAGE EXACTLY.
```

## USAGE

1. Get product grid (Product Grid Skill)
2. Identify brand tone (nurturing vs luxury vs clinical)
3. Select matching expression template
4. Fill in: model description, product details, backdrop color
5. Generate in Higgsfield/Nano Banana with grid as reference
6. Check: expression matches brand, product correct, skin realistic
