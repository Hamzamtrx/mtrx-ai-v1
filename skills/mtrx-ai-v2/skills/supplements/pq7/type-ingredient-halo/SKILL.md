## Language Guidelines

### Words They Use (DO use):
- "Food noise" / "food chatter"
- "Can't stop thinking about food"
- "The radio that won't turn off"
- "3pm crash" / "afternoon slump"
- "Blood sugar crash"
- "Willpower isn't working"
- "What used to work doesn't work anymore"
- "My body changed"
- "Peace of mind" / "mental quiet"

### Words to AVOID:
- "Cravings" (too generic - use "food noise")
- "Appetite suppressant" (sounds like a drug)
- "Burn fat" / "melt pounds" (scam language)
- "Revolutionary" / "breakthrough" (skeptics hate this)
- "Game changer" / "life changing" (overused)
- "Proprietary blend" (red flag for skeptics)
