# TYPE: Benefit Checklist

## Purpose

Clean product shot with a checkmark benefit list. Direct, clear value proposition. Product on one side, benefits on the other, strong headline, CTA button, and trust element.

**Best for:** Direct response, comparison angles (vs competitors, vs alternatives), objection handling, clear value communication.

---

## Required Inputs

```
PRODUCT_NAME: [e.g., "PQ7"]
PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION: [e.g., "teal pouch with pink/green splash design"]
BRAND_NAME: [e.g., "Primal Queen"]
CAPSULE_STYLE: [e.g., "two tan/beige capsules" - CRITICAL: must match actual product]

ACCENT_COLOR: [e.g., "pink"]
TEXT_COLOR_1: [e.g., "dark purple"]
TEXT_COLOR_2: [e.g., "pink"]
BACKGROUND: [e.g., "soft light pink/lavender"]

AVATAR: [Target avatar]
H1_LINE_1: [First line of headline]
H1_LINE_2: [Second line of headline - optional]
BENEFITS: [4 checkmark benefits]
CTA: [Button text]
TRUST: [e.g., "365-Day Money Back Guarantee"]
```

---

## Prompt Template

```
Clean supplement product photography, [ASPECT_RATIO] aspect ratio. Soft [BACKGROUND] background.

LEFT SIDE: [PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION] ([BRAND_NAME] [PRODUCT_NAME] branding) with [CAPSULE_STYLE] at the base.

RIGHT SIDE: Benefit checklist with [ACCENT_COLOR] checkmarks:
✓ [BENEFIT_1]
✓ [BENEFIT_2]
✓ [BENEFIT_3]
✓ [BENEFIT_4]

TOP TEXT: Large bold headline "[H1_LINE_1]" with second line "[H1_LINE_2]" - mix of [TEXT_COLOR_1] and [TEXT_COLOR_2] text.

BOTTOM: Rounded [ACCENT_COLOR] button with white text: "[CTA]"

Below button: "★★★★★ [TRUST]" in small [ACCENT_COLOR] text.

Clean, minimal, modern aesthetic. Sans-serif typography. Professional supplement brand look.

[ASPECT_RATIO] aspect ratio.
```

---

## Copy Structure

```
H1 (her situation)
    ↓
Checklist (answers her objections)
    ↓
CTA (the resolution)
    ↓
Trust (removes risk)
```

**The Logic:**
- H1 hooks her with HER words about HER problem
- Checklist answers the specific objections SHE has
- CTA promises the outcome SHE wants
- Trust removes the final barrier
