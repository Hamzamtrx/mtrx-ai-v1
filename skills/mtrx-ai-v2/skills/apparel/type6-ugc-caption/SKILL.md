# Type 6: UGC / Model with Caption

## Overview

UGC-style static ads featuring a male model wearing the product in real-world settings with handwritten-style caption overlay. Designed to look like authentic user-generated content, not polished advertising.

## When to Use

- Native social feed ads (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook)
- Scroll-stopping content that blends with organic posts
- Testimonial/review style messaging
- Relatable "real guy" energy

## Visual Style

### Caption Style: Handwritten Marker (VALIDATED)

- White marker/paint pen style text
- Slightly messy, casual, like someone drew on the photo
- Placed in upper portion of frame, angled slightly
- NOT clean sans-serif, NOT in a bar/box

### Model

- Male, mid 30s
- Athletic build (arms show shirt fit)
- Confident but not cocky expression
- Slight smirk, relaxed energy
- Real skin texture (pores, stubble, imperfections)

### Environment Requirements

Must feel REAL, not stock photo:
- Dirty/smudged mirrors
- Random objects (gym bag, water bottle, toothbrush)
- Imperfect lighting
- Lived-in spaces

### Camera Quality

- iPhone selfie aesthetic
- Slight grain acceptable
- Not overly sharp or professional
- Natural phone camera look

## Reference Image Required

Always use the 3x3 product grid as reference to capture:
- Small logo placement (bottom right of shirt)
- Exact shirt texture/fabric look
- Proper fit (hugs arms, fitted chest)

---

## Voice Guidelines (UndrDog Tone)

### DO:
- Short punchy sentences
- Proof/data ("100 washes", "3 months", "lifetime guarantee")
- Direct, aggressive, fed-up energy
- Working class masculine voice
- Mild profanity acceptable ("damn", "f'ing")

### DON'T:
- Flowery or soft language
- "Obsessed" / "game changer" / "life changing"
- Overly enthusiastic exclamation marks
- Feminine UGC phrases
- Long sentences

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Handwritten white marker text (not clean font)
- [ ] Text in upper portion, slightly angled
- [ ] Real environment (not stock photo clean)
- [ ] iPhone selfie quality (not professional)
- [ ] Shirt logo visible (if using reference)
- [ ] Real skin texture (pores, stubble)
- [ ] Caption matches UndrDog voice
- [ ] 9:16 vertical format
